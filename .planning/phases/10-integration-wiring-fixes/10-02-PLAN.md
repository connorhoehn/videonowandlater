<objective>
Remove the legacy `RecordingEndRule` EventBridge rule from session-stack.ts. Two rules currently match the same IVS Recording State Change event and both target the same Lambda, causing duplicate invocations and DynamoDB version-conflict errors on every recording end event.

Purpose: Eliminate double-invocation of recording-ended Lambda that causes race conditions in DynamoDB writes. The correct `RecordingEndRuleV2` (using `event_name` filter) remains. The legacy rule (using deprecated `recording_status` filter) is removed.
Output: Single EventBridge rule targeting recording-ended Lambda. CDK deploy required to apply the change.
</objective>
