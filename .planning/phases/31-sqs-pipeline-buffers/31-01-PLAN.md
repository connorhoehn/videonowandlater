**recording-ended (3 rules → 1 queue with explicit multi-rule policy):**

Replace all three `addTarget` calls from `targets.LambdaFunction(recordingEndedFn, ...)` to `targets.SqsQueue(recordingEndedQueue)`:
```typescript
this.recordingEndRule.addTarget(new targets.SqsQueue(recordingEndedQueue));
stageRecordingEndRule.addTarget(new targets.SqsQueue(recordingEndedQueue));
recordingRecoveryRule.addTarget(new targets.SqsQueue(recordingEndedQueue));
```

Since `targets.SqsQueue` auto-adds a per-rule policy, three separate policy statements will be added by CDK. This is valid. Do NOT also call `recordingEndedQueue.addToResourcePolicy` manually — that would duplicate the policy statements.
