<objective>
Fix the PIPE-06 trap where scan-stuck-sessions permanently excludes sessions stuck in transcriptStatus='processing'. Add a transcriptStatusUpdatedAt timestamp to every transcriptStatus transition in session-repository.ts, then add a 2-hour stale-processing gate to the scan-stuck-sessions eligibility filter.

Purpose: HARD-04 — a session where MediaConvert or Transcribe threw AFTER setting status='processing' can never be recovered by the existing cron. With the 2h threshold, sessions stuck for more than 2 hours are eligible for recovery even if transcriptStatus='processing'.
Output: updateTranscriptStatus writes timestamps; scan-stuck-sessions recovers stale-processing sessions; tests verify both branches.
